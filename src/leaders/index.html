LEADERS

Alin Tase

TEACHING PASTOR

    Alin Tase is originally from Romania and is an engineer by profession, working in the Oil & Gas industry. He graduated in 2007 from "Politehnica" University, in the city of Bucharest. Two years later he also earned a Master's degree in Electromagnetic Compatibility from the same institute. Alin moved to The Netherlands in 2013 and became a Christian one year later. For almost seven years he was a member of Trinity International Church in Leidschendam, where he served as an Elder for three years and was also a member of the worship team.

    During this time he felt that God was calling him to serve in ministry. Also it was on his heart to plant a new church, close to The Hague area, where he currently lives. By God's grace and providence, in the summer of 2024 the doors were opened when a family -Tom and Renate Joseph- offered their place to establish a new congregation. This is how Life in Christ Community Church (LCCC) started.

    As the teaching pastor of LCCC, Alin has a deep passion for the Word of God and his great desire is to see Gospel-saturated churches all over The Netherlands and Europe.

    Alin has been married since 2013 to Andrea Hernandez from Mexico, and together they have three daughters: Cristina, Emilia, and Ariana.
